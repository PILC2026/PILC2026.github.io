PILC 2026 — Conference Information

Participate

PILC 2026 brings together global experts to explore where talent development, technological progress, and brand power converge. This year’s theme, Human-Centric Leadership: Talent, Technology, and Brand Power, focuses on how leadership is transformed when organizations place people at the center while embracing technological advancements and brand authenticity.

Dates

26 – 28 March 2026

Venue

PAR University of Applied Sciences
Trg Riječke rezolucije 4, Rijeka, Croatia

Format

Hybrid (In-person + Online)
Conference Schedule: TBA

Call for Papers

Authors are invited to prepare papers addressing one or several interrelated thematic areas within the conference’s main topic. Contributions may include theoretical insights, empirical research, practical case studies, and interactive discussions.
