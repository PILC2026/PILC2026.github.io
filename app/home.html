PAR International Leadership Conference

PILC 2026

PILC 2026 brings together global experts to explore where talent development, technological progress, and brand power converge. This year’s theme, Human-Centric Leadership: Talent, Technology, and Brand Power, focuses on how leadership is transformed when organizations place people at the center while embracing technological advancements and brand authenticity.

In an era of rapid digital transformation, shifting workforce values, and rising demands for authentic, purpose-driven organizations, the importance of human-centric leadership has never been greater. This conference will empower attendees to understand and develop the capabilities modern leaders need, from cultivating talent and leveraging emerging technologies to building strong, resilient, and trustworthy brands.

Authors are invited to prepare papers addressing one or several interrelated thematic areas within the conference’s main topic. The interdisciplinary approach promotes the exchange of ideas among representatives from academia, business leaders across diverse industries, and students. Conference participants are encouraged to contribute fresh perspectives and present theoretical insights, empirical research, practical case studies, and interactive discussions through their papers, presentations, and dialogue sessions.

Experience three days of thought-provoking discussions on leading with purpose and reimagining leadership for the human side of the digital age. Join us at the PILC Conference 2026, where leaders of tomorrow meet today.
